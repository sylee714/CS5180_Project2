    Graduation Pledge Programs

    The Graduation Pledge programs at Cal Poly Pomona can help you stay on track towards a timely graduation. The programs support incoming students in earning a bachelor's degree within two (for transfer students) or four years (for freshmen). 

    CPP offers the California Promise as its graduation pledge, a legislatively-mandated program. Students who are eligible for the California Promise promise to balance school, work and personal responsibilities and stay committed to their education. CPP will provide an effective and coordinated advising program to help students register for the appropriate classes and stay on track to graduate. Graduation Pledge students receive priority registration appointments for the fall, winter, spring and summer terms.

    Students who are eligible for California Promise will receive an invitation to apply. To learn more about our 2-year and 4-year programs, click on the links in the lefthand navigation list.
