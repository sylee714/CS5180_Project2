Passing of Campus Leader in Student Affairs

Posted on May 7, 2020

A message was shared with the campus community on May 7 about the recent passing of Kevin Colaner, associate vice president and chief of operations in the Division of Student Affairs.

This post was updated on 6/19 to include information about the Kevin Colaner Memorial Scholarship.

A Message to the Campus Community

To the campus community,

It is with great difficulty and sadness that we write to you today. This morning, we learned that we have lost a beloved colleague, leader and champion for students in Kevin Colaner, associate vice president and chief of operations in the Division of Student Affairs.

Our thoughts are with his family and friends during this difficult time.

This is a devastating loss for our campus community and especially the students he has enthusiastically served throughout his career. Since he joined Cal Poly Pomona in 2008, Kevin has brought dynamic energy, creativity, leadership and a deep commitment to diversity and inclusion.

His leadership has seen us through countless challenges, transitions and complex situations. Knowing that we are stronger when we come together, he worked hard to build bridges with humor and authenticity. Kevin had the ability to bring out the best of us. We know that we are not alone in being shocked and heartbroken by this news. We will miss him deeply.

Kevin’s unexpected passing has been extremely difficult for his family, and they’ve asked us to respect their privacy and not share private information. We will respect their wishes and reach out them on an individual basis. In light of concerns regarding the coronavirus pandemic, his family has confirmed that his passing was not due to Covid-19.

We know this announcement will cause distress and grief. As a campus, we mourn together, and we support one another. If you are hurting, please reach out and talk with a counselor. Information about support services is at the end of this message.

We are planning a virtual memorial event as a campus community and will share details when they are available. We will not soon forget Kevin’s love and deep commitment for our students and this campus.

Sincerely,

Megan M. Stang, Ed.D.
Administrator in Charge for Student Affairs

Soraya M. Coley, Ph.D.
President

Counseling and Support Services
Students may reach out to Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS). We have counselors available to speak with students who may need immediate grief support. The phone number is 909-869-3220. After-hours and weekend crisis support is also available by calling the CAPS main line and pressing “2.”

Faculty and staff may contact CPPLifeMatters by Empathia for short-term grief counseling and/or referral to local resources. CPPLifeMatters is available on a live basis, 24 hours a day/7days a week at 800-367-7474 and at CPPLifeMatters by Empathia.

Memorial Scholarship

The Division of Student Affairs intends to establish the Kevin Colaner Memorial Scholarship, which will keep his legacy alive by supporting the many students to be aided in his honor. Visit the Kevin Colaner Memorial Scholarship page to donate. Those who wish to mail in their contributions can send a check payable to the Cal Poly Pomona Philanthropic Foundation to Cal Poly Pomona Gift Processing, Attention: Student Affairs/Honor of Dr. Colaner, P.O. Box 3121, Pomona, CA 91769.
