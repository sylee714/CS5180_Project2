Welcome to Bronco Bucks

 

Get login process

If you are already an active CPP student/staff you are already enrolled in GET. Just click one of the GET buttons below to log in. If you need more information, please view our "How to GET Started" section below.

 

What is the Bronco Access Card

Your Bronco Access Card is your Cal Poly Pomona ID. The magnetic strip is compatible with card readers at most campus dining service and convenience store locations, the Bronco Bookstore, Farm Store, ASI Box Office, and computer labs. To fill your Bronco Access Card with Bronco Bucks, all you have to do is register with GET and deposit money. It's that easy!

What is a Bronco Bucks Account

Bronco Bucks is a declining balance account that functions much like a regular bank debit account. You (or parents) simply deposit money into your Bronco Bucks account using GET (Formerly ManageMyID), so you can make on-campus purchases using your Bronco Access Card.

What is GET

Bronco Bucks is managed by GET, an online management system which gives you 24/7 access to your account. GET offers three convenient functions: GET Funds, GET Food, and GET Photo.


GET FUNDS

With GET Funds, you can:

  • Add Bronco Bucks to your card
  • View your account balance and transaction history
  • Report your card lost or stolen
  • Request funds from parents or guardians

 

 

How to GET Started

You are already registered, but the following steps are neccessary:

  • You will need your Bronco Name* and Bronco Direct password
  • Visit the GET website
  • You will be re-directed to the Cal Poly Pomona Online Services log in page
  • Enter your Bronco Name and Password
  • You will be directed to the GET website
  • Manage your account—deposit funds, order food, upload your photo, and more!

    *Bronco Name is your email address without the @cpp.edu. Example: If you email is "myname@cpp.edu", you would just enter "myname" for Bronco Name.

Our Commitment

The Cal Poly Pomona Foundation, Inc., established in 1966, is an integral component of the educational mission of the University. In pursuit of this mission, the Foundation is a partner in the University community that includes students, faculty, staff, administrators, alumni and members of the larger community. The Foundation exists to provide the highest level of service and financial support while maintaining corporate fiscal integrity. [ Read More About The Foundation's Commitment ]
