 Important Information: COVID - 19 (coronavirus) and SSEP (remote work/virtual services)

In line with University gudiance, SSEP is taking the following precautions to protect our community while continuing our daily activities in ways that are appropriate under the circumstances. 

  • Beginning Wednesday, 3/18/2020 all in-person services will be paused until further notice. Our office will be closed but all staff are working remotely and offering virtual student services.
  • We encourage the use of email to connect with our SSEP staff. Our staff will be closely monitoring emails to respond as quickly as possible.
  • Please refer to our staff directory for information: https://www.cpp.edu/ssep/about-us/staff.shtml

STAY CONNECTED & FOLLOW US @EOP_CPP

Welcome to the Student Support and Equity Programs (SSEP) website! As part of the Division of Student Affairs SSEP is dedicated to making a positive difference in the lives of students through high quality programs and services that promote educational opportunity and support student learning and success. SSEP is the home for three key programs: the Educational Opportunity Program (EOP) including our transitional programs Summer Bridge and Transfer Bridge; Renaissance Scholars, and the Veterans Resource Center.
