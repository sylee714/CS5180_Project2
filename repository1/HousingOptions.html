Housing Options

University Housing Services offers three living communities - Residential Halls, Residential Suites, and The Lyle Center for Regenerative Studies. 

Within the Residential Halls and Suites there are Theme Living Communities  with social and educational events and workshops every semester. Residents have an opportunity to engage with their peers and within the campus community. Topics range from multiculturalism & diversity to personal development student health and wellbeing.
