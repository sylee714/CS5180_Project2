    Pre-Admission Advising

    Prospective Broncos from an international institution should contact the Office of Admissions for pre-admission advising. Our Outreach counselors assist prospective students with pre-admission advising and can answer questions regarding the admissions process to Cal Poly Pomona, requirements, information about majors and programs, and details regarding important deadlines. Contact our office to schedule an appointment at 909-869-3529, or email  outreach@cpp.edu, or fill our contact request form. Daily virtual drop-in advising is available! Please see the links below.

    If you have coursework or hold a degree from an international institution, please contact the Office of Admissions.
