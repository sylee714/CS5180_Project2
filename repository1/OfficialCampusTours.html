    Cal Poly Pomona Official Campus Tours

    Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the Office of Outreach, Recruitment and Educational Partnerships has moved to remote service. Although we are not offering on-campus tours at this time, we welcome you to attend a live virtual tour, given by a student ambassador who will share information about the campus, events and student services. You will also learn about Cal Poly Pomona’s uniqueness, including our campus’ rich legacy and diversity, and you will hear first-hand accounts about our learn-by-doing approach. 

    NOTE: For disability-related accommodations, please contact outreach@cpp.edu. Please provide us with as much lead time as possible to accommodate your live session request.
