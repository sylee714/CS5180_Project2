    About OREP

    The Office of Outreach, Recruitment, and Educational Partnerships (OREP) encompass pre-admission advising, campus tours and outreach. Our Outreach staff attends various events, including college fairs and classroom visits to provide prospective students with information to determine whether Cal Poly Pomona will fit their educational goals. The OREP staff are available to assist prospective students to understand the application process, requirements for admissions, and details regarding impacted, and non-impacted programs. If you have coursework or hold a degree from an international institution, please visit the Office of Admissions to learn more. OREP counselors are available by appointment for pre-admission advising Monday - Friday from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Additionally, we also offer daily drop-in virtual advising to get general questions answered immediately. Please see further information at the bottom of this page for the link to join. To schedule an appointment, email outreach@cpp.edu.

    COVID-19 (Coronavirus) Service Update

    The Office of Outreach, Recruitment & Educational Partnerships has moved to remote service given the health concern with COVID-19. Our staff is working diligently to still provide our services virtually to prospective students.
