    First Year Experience Showcase

    RSVP Now!

    Join us for the virtual FYE Showcase on Tuesday, December 1, 2020, hosted by the Learn Through Discovery and First Year Experience teams. See the first-year students in action as they present their Signature Polytechnic Experiences (PolyX)!

    LTD FYE Challenge participants will present their innovative solutions based on this year's common read book, "Occasional Magic: True Stories About Defying the Impossible."

    There will also be a PolyX Projects poster session, announcement of the FYE essay contest winners, and a virtual scavenger hunt! See schedule below.

    RSVP to FYE Showcase

    Schedule

    Time Event
    11 am - 11:50 am FYE Challenge
    11:50 pm - 12:50 pm FYE PolyX Projects Showcase
    12:50 pm - 1:15 pm
    Announcement of Winners for the Essay Contest, FYE Challenge, and Scavenger Hunt
