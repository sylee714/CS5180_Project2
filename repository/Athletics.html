    Athletics

    No conference activity is planned for fall 2020. The CCAA conference is developing return to play practice and competition schedules that would begin in January 2021. Once approved, the campus will follow a phased return plan that addresses staff, student-athletes and facilities. 

    The primary goal of the Department of Intercollegiate Athletics, a member of the California Collegiate Athletic Association (CCAA), is to effectively execute a plan focused on health and safety for professional staff and student-athletes to return to work and activity with a focus on physical, mental, and social well-being related to participation in intercollegiate athletics. Guidelines were developed based on considerations from the American College Health Association (ACHA), the National Athletic Trainers Association Intercollegiate Council for Sports Medicine (NATAICSM), Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), and following the Core Principles the National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA) Sport Science Institute.  

    Visit Bronco Athletics for additional information.
